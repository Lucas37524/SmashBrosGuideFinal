Mario

In Ultimate, Mario is currently ranked 21st out of 82 characters on the tier list, which places him in the A+ tier at the top of high tier. This is slightly lower than his position in Smash 4, where he was ranked 9th out of 54 characters and was considered to be at the lower end of top tier. One of Mario's biggest strengths in both games is his effective frame data. This means that his moves have little startup lag and relatively low end lag, making it difficult for opponents to punish him. Additionally, Mario's combo game is quite flexible, allowing him to rack up damage quickly and reliably, particularly with his up air ladders.
