Link

Link is a slow heavyweight fighter with the Master Sword, granting him many ranged attacks. His special moves include Boomerang, a swift returning attack, Spin Attack, a potent move with weak recovery, and Bomb, which can be thrown or placed on the ground. Link's weaknesses have placed him in the C tier, ranking 11th out of 12 on the tier list.
